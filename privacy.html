Privacy Policy

R&R Asset Management is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care, discretion, and integrity. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data when you use our website or contact us in relation to our services.

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including your name, email address, telephone number, company details, and any other information you choose to share through our contact forms, email correspondence, or service enquiries.

We may also collect limited technical information automatically through your use of our website, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.

Your information may be used to:

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable under UK data protection law, we process personal data on the basis of legitimate interests, contractual necessity, legal obligation, or your consent, depending on the nature of the interaction and the information involved.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers, professional advisers, or regulatory authorities where this is necessary for the operation of our business, the provision of our services, or compliance with legal obligations.

Any such sharing is carried out with appropriate safeguards and only where necessary.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as is reasonably necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy legal, regulatory, tax, accounting, or reporting requirements.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, misuse, loss, disclosure, or alteration. While we take reasonable steps to secure data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request erasure, object to or restrict certain processing, and withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
